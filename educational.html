My academic Journey

I have attended three schools so far in my academic journey. I began my primary education at SK Pantai Pitas (2011-2016). Continued to SMK Kanibungan Pitas for my secondary studies (2017-2022), and then enrolled in SMK Pitas II for my Form 6 (STPM) education (2022-2024). Currently, I am pursuing a Degree in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi Mara (UiTM), starting in 2024.

Primary Education

My primary education at SK Pantai laid the foundation for my academic journey. During these early years, I developed basic literacy, numeracy, and communication skills. It was also where I learned the importance of discipline, teamwork, and curiosity in learning. This stage played a crucial role in shaping my confidence and eagerness to explore new knowledge.

Secondary Education

During my time at SMK Kanibungan, I was placed in the Sport Science stream, where I focused on subjects related to physical education, health, and human biology. This streamhelped me understand the science behind sports and physical performance, while also encouraging a healthy and active lifestyle. It also taugh me discipline, teamwork, and the importance of both mental and physical well-being.

Post-secondary Education

I continued my Post-secondary education at SMK Pitas II, where I was placed in the Arts stream. This class allowed me to focus on subjects such as Visual Arts and also other subject about Geography and Malay Literature. Through this experience, I developed creative thinking, analytical skills, and a deeper appreciation for culture and expression. It also helped strengthen my communication and writing abilities, which have been valuable in my current field of study.
